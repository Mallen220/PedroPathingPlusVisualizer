### What's New!

## **Features:**

### **Printable Strategy Sheet**

- **Match-Ready Guides:** Generate a clean, printable overview of your autonomous routine.
- **Includes Everything:** Automatically compiles a field map, path statistics, and the full command sequence.
- **Perfect for Collaboration:** Easily share your strategy with alliance partners and drive teams.
- **How to use:** Go to **Export > Strategy Sheet** in the top toolbar.

### **Telemetry Visualization**

- **Import Real-World Data:** Overlay actual robot telemetry logs directly onto your simulation.
- **Compare & Improve:** Visualizing the difference between your planned path and actual execution helps you tune your robot's performance.
- **Ghost Robot:** Watch a "ghost" robot replay the recorded motion alongside your autonomous routine.
- **Flexible Formats:** Supports standard CSV and JSON log files.
- **Access it:** Open the **File Manager** and click the **Import Telemetry** button.

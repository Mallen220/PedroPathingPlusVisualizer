### What's New!

## **Features:**

### **Enhanced Validation Controls**

Take more control over path safety with new validation tools.

- **Manual Validation:** Press **`Shift + V`** to instantly check your path for collisions and issues.
- **Continuous Validation Toggle:** Quickly toggle real-time validation on or off with **`Alt + V`**.
- **Field Boundaries:** New option to warn if the robot exits the field boundary (enable in **Settings > Robot**).
- **Restrict Dragging:** Prevent points from being dragged outside the field area (enable in **Settings > Robot**).

### **Other Changes**

- **Microsoft Store Prep:** Continued improvements for Microsoft Store submission, including privacy policy integration.
